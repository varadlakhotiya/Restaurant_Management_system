About Us

Welcome to Spice Symphony, where the rich and diverse flavors of India come alive. Since our inception in 2024, we have been dedicated to delivering an unparalleled dining experience that combines tradition, innovation, and hospitality. Our passion for authentic Indian cuisine is reflected in every dish we serve, crafted with love and the finest ingredients.

Our Story

Spice Symphony was born out of a dream to share the vibrant culinary heritage of India with the world. Founded by Varad Lakhotiya, a lifelong food enthusiast, our restaurant has grown from a humble family kitchen to a celebrated dining destination in Auranagabd,Maharashtra. Over the years, we have stayed true to our roots while embracing modern techniques to create a unique culinary experience.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To celebrate the art of Indian cooking by delivering authentic flavors with a contemporary twist. We are committed to using sustainable, locally sourced ingredients and fostering a sense of community through food.

Vision: To be recognized as a global ambassador of Indian cuisine, known for our innovation, exceptional service, and dedication to preserving culinary traditions.

Meet Our Culinary Experts

At the heart of Spice Symphony is our talented team, led by Sarika Lakhotiya & Rupali Lakhotiya, a culinary visionary with over 15 years of experience. Chef Rajesh has trained under some of India's most renowned chefs and brings a wealth of knowledge and creativity to our kitchen. Together with our passionate staff, we strive to make every meal a memorable one.
